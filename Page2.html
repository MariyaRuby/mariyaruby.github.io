MALL EVENTS

The Dalton Mall hosts events throughout the year to bring people together in the Mall to engage, entertain and inspire our community. Whether you are looking to participate in an event or seeking family friendly activities, we hope you will explore all that is happening at the Dalton Mall.

COLOR FOR A CAUSE

MARCH

Color for a Cause is a live mural coloring contest and shopping event that raises funds and awareness for local organizations. Shop anywhere in the Mall during the event and show your receipt to support the cause of your choice. This fun night out at the Mall brings together hundreds of people in support of a variety of important causes.
